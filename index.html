01 / The Club

Half a century of green fire.

Founded in 1974 by railway workers of the Žalgrila district, ZMFC — Žalgrilska Milicija Football Club — has been the beating heart of its neighborhood for over fifty years. Our dragon crest was chosen in 1981 to represent the fire we bring to every match.

We currently compete in the Second Bosnian Division, chasing promotion with a squad built on local talent, hardened veterans, and a philosophy the ultras call "disciplina i vatra" — discipline and fire.

  • DisciplinaOrder on and off the pitch.
  • VatraPlay like the crest is burning.
  • ZajednicaCommunity first, always.
